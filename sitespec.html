Visit Havana

Subject

A comprehensive travel guide for exploring the vibrant and culturally rich city of Havana, Cuba.

Description

Havana Gateway is a website dedicated to providing tourists and travelers with all the essential information they need to make the most of their visit to Havana. Whether they are a history enthusiast, a food lover, or simply looking to immerse themselves in the unique atmosphere of Havana, this guide aims to be the go-to resource.

Purpose

The primary purpose of Havana Gateway is to offer valuable insights and tips to travelers interested in exploring Havana. This website aims to:

  • Provide in-depth information about Havana's history, culture, and attractions.
  • Offer practical travel advice, including accommodation options, transportation, and safety tips.
  • Highlight the best places to experience Havana's vibrant music and nightlife scene.

Target audience

Havana Gateway is designed for travelers planning a visit to Havana, Cuba. The target audience includes:

  • Tourists interested in exploring Havana's unique history and culture.
  • Food enthusiasts looking to discover Cuban cuisine and dining experiences.
  • Music and dance lovers eager to immerse themselves in Havana's vibrant music scene.
  • Travelers seeking practical information and tips for a smooth and enjoyable trip to Havana.

Content of the website

  1. Home Page
    • Hero: Havana Capitol image, with menu and pages links.
    • About Havana: A brief introduction about Havana's history.
    • Places to visit: Must-see Havana attractions, with a button to see more.
    • Insider tips: Useful information for travelers about the best time to visit, things to know, and money and costs tips.
    • Footer: Includes links to essential sections like "About and Contact," "Copyright" and "Sitespecs"
  2. About Havana
    • A page dedicated to Havana colonial and revolution history, with pictures and links to external information.
  3. Places to visit
    • Features all Havana attractions, a description and pictures.
  4. Insider tips
    • A page for tips and tricks about traveling to Havana.
  5. Contact
    • Contact Form: Includes a contact form for inquiries, feedback, or collaboration opportunities.
    • Social Media Links: Provides links to all social media profiles available.
