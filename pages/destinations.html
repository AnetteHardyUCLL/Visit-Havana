Habana Vieja

Old Havana, with its colorful streets and colonial architecture, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Discover the vibrant culture, visit historic landmarks, and savor the local cuisine.

Gran Teatro de La Habana

Alicia Alonso's Theater is a cultural gem in Havana, known for world-class ballet and dance performances. Experience the artistry of Cuban dancers in a grand and elegant setting.

El Capitolio

El Capitolio, an iconic landmark in the heart of Havana, is a symbol of the city's grandeur. Take a guided tour to learn about its history and the Cuban government's functions.

El Vedado

El Vedado is a modern and lively neighborhood in Havana, known for its nightlife, restaurants, and cultural venues. Explore its streets to get a taste of contemporary Cuban life.

Habana del Este

Habana del Este beach is a paradise for beach lovers. Relax on its sandy shores, swim in the turquoise waters, and soak up the sun in this beautiful coastal area.

El Malec칩n

El Malecon is a picturesque seawall that stretches along the Havana coastline. Take a leisurely walk, enjoy the sea breeze, and witness the social life of the locals at sunset.

Catedral de San Cristobal

Plaza de la Catedral is a charming square in Old Havana, home to the stunning Catedral de San Cristobal. Explore the surrounding streets and admire the beauty of this historic area.

Plaza Vieja

La Plaza Vieja, or Old Square, is a beautifully restored plaza in Havana with colorful buildings and lively cafes. It's a delightful place to explore, relax, and soak in the atmosphere.
