La Casa del Café

La Casa del Café is a charming coffee haven in the heart of Havana, where you can savor a diverse selection of coffee blends and products. Nestled in Vedado, it offers a warm and inviting atmosphere for coffee enthusiasts.

Almacenes San José

Almacenes San José, a captivating shopping destination, beckons you to delve into a diverse array of artisanal and Cuban-made crafts. Situated in picturesque Havana, this marketplace offers a splendid opportunity to immerse yourself in local craftsmanship, ensuring a rewarding shopping experience.

La Casa del Chocolate

La Casa del Chocolate is a true paradise for chocolate lovers. Located in the heart of Havana, it is renowned for its mouthwatering Cuban chocolate products, providing an irresistible treat for your taste buds.

Clandestina

Clandestina is a unique and trendy shopping destination that caters to fashion-forward individuals seeking distinctive Cuban products. Situated in the historic streets of Old Havana, it showcases contemporary Cuban design and style, ensuring a one-of-a-kind shopping experience.

La Casa del Habano

La Casa del Habano is a sanctuary for connoisseurs of fine cigars. This distinguished shop offers a premium selection of Cuban cigars, creating an authentic Cuban experience that leaves a lasting impression on aficionados and newcomers alike.

Memorias Librería

Memorias Librería, a paradise for book enthusiasts, hosts a diverse collection of books in the heart of Havana. From Cuban literature to international classics, it offers a retreat for literary exploration, establishing itself as a must-visit destination for book lovers.

Linen clothing, street vendors

Immerse yourself in the vibrant tapestry of Cuban culture as you explore an enticing array of locally crafted linen clothing and accessories. Havana's street vendors offer a diverse selection of handmade items, allowing you to discover unique pieces and embrace the local artisanal spirit.

Old Havana, street vendors

Old Havana's bustling streets come alive with vibrant street vendors, where you can delve into the kaleidoscope of colors, sounds, and flavors. Explore the lively markets to uncover a wide variety of Cuban crafts, souvenirs, and local treasures.

Habana 1791

Habana 1791 entices you with its exquisite collection of Cuban fragrances and perfumes, encapsulating the very essence of Havana's history. This inviting spot welcomes you to immerse yourself in the scents that reflect the city's rich heritage and charm.
