Restaurante Antojos

Restaurante Antojos is a charming eatery that offers a delightful fusion of Cuban and international cuisine. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere, live music, and an extensive menu that caters to all tastes.

La Guarida

La Guarida is a renowned restaurant located in the heart of Old Havana. It's known for its elegant setting, gourmet cuisine, and the beautiful views from its rooftop terrace.

El Cocinero

El Cocinero is a trendy restaurant set in an old cooking oil factory's chimney. It's a hotspot for contemporary Cuban cuisine and artistic events in Havana's Vedado district.

La Bodeguita del Medio

La Bodeguita del Medio is a historic bar and restaurant famous for its mojitos and its connections to renowned figures like Ernest Hemingway. It's a must-visit in Old Havana.

El Floridita

El Floridita, also known as the "Cradle of the Daiquiri," is an iconic bar in Havana. It's a favorite of Hemingway and serves some of the best cocktails in the city.

La Casa del Gelato

La Casa del Gelato is the perfect place to cool down with delicious gelato and other frozen treats. Located in Habana del Este, it's a sweet escape by the beach.

Fábrica de Arte Cubano

Fábrica de Arte Cubano (FAC) is a unique cultural space in Havana. It's a blend of art gallery, music venue, and nightclub, offering a diverse cultural experience in the Vedado neighborhood.

Paladar Doña Eutimia

Paladar Doña Eutimia is a charming paladar (private restaurant) near Plaza de la Catedral. It's known for its traditional Cuban cuisine and authentic flavors.

Casa Mía Paladar

Casa Mía Paladar is a hidden gem located near La Plaza Vieja. It's a cozy eatery with a focus on Cuban and Mediterranean dishes, providing a unique dining experience.
