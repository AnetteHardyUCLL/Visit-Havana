Fábrica de Arte Cubano

Fábrica de Arte Cubano (FAC) is a one-of-a-kind cultural space in Havana. It's a dynamic fusion of art gallery, music venue, and nightlife hotspot, offering a diverse and vibrant cultural experience in the heart of Vedado.

Gran Teatro de La Habana

The Gran Teatro de la Habana, or Grand Theater of Havana, is an iconic cultural institution. It's renowned for its stunning architecture and world-class performances, including ballet, opera, and more.

Tropicana Nightclub

Tropicana Nightclub is a legendary entertainment venue in Havana. Known for its spectacular cabaret shows and outdoor performances, it offers a taste of Cuban nightlife and showmanship.

Jazz Club La Zorra y El Cuervo

Jazz Club La Zorra y El Cuervo is a hidden gem for jazz enthusiasts. Located in Old Havana, this intimate club hosts exceptional live jazz performances and a cozy ambiance for music lovers.

El Guajirito

El Guajirito is a vibrant Cuban restaurant and entertainment venue. It offers traditional Cuban cuisine along with live music, allowing you to immerse yourself in the island's rich culture while enjoying delicious food and captivating performances.

Casa de la Música

Casa de la Música is a renowned music venue in Havana, attracting top Cuban bands and musicians. Dance the night away to the infectious rhythms of salsa, son, and more in a vibrant setting.

EL Gato Tuerto

EL Gato Tuerto, or The One-Eyed Cat, is a historic cabaret and music club in Havana. It's a beloved institution for jazz, bolero, and trova music, offering a glimpse into Cuba's musical heritage.

Submarino Amarillo

Submarino Amarillo is a trendy spot for music and drinks. Located in Vedado, it's known for its vibrant nightlife and live performances, making it a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Don Cangrejo

Don Cangrejo, the daytime restaurant of the Cuban fisheries, transforms into a lively party hub, especially on Friday nights. With alfresco live music, often featuring big-name acts, it exudes an energetic atmosphere reminiscent of an undergraduate freshers' ball.
