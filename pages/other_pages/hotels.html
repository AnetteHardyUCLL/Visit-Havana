Hotel Inglaterra

Nestled in the heart of Vedado, Hotel Inglaterra stands as an iconic and centrally located hotel in Havana. With a rich history, it offers guests a comfortable and convenient base for their Havana experience. Enjoy well-appointed rooms, excellent amenities, and easy access to the city's attractions.

Iberostar Parque Central

Iberostar Parque Central is a symbol of luxury in Havana. This opulent hotel is situated in the heart of the city, providing guests with a lavish and comfortable lodging experience. From its elegant rooms to its top-notch amenities, this hotel is a perfect choice for those seeking a premium stay in Havana.

Hotel Nacional

Experience elegance and sophistication at Hotel Nacional. This luxurious hotel in Havana seamlessly blends style with comfort, offering guests a taste of grandeur in the heart of the city. Immerse yourself in the historical charm of this iconic establishment while enjoying the finest accommodations.

Iberostar Grand Packard

Iberostar Grand Packard is the epitome of luxury in Havana. With its refined ambiance and top-tier service, this hotel ensures a lavish and memorable stay for guests. Relax in its well-appointed rooms and indulge in the amenities that make this hotel a standout choice for discerning travelers.

Hotel Meliá Habana

Hotel Meliá Habana is a haven of comfort in Havana. This hotel offers guests a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, making it an ideal choice for a pleasant stay. Explore the city by day and return to the relaxation and comfort of this charming hotel in the evening.

Gran Hotel Manzana Kempinski

Gran Hotel Manzana Kempinski offers a luxurious setting and access to Havana's vibrant music scene. As a guest, you'll enjoy a premium experience with beautifully appointed rooms, exceptional service, and access to the city's vibrant culture. This hotel is perfect for those seeking a lavish stay in the heart of Havana.

Hotel Saratoga

Hotel Saratoga is a timeless gem of elegance nestled in the heart of Havana. With its classic charm and contemporary amenities, this iconic hotel provides guests with a truly unforgettable experience. Immerse yourself in the allure of this historic establishment while enjoying the highest level of comfort and sophistication that Havana has to offer.

Malecón 663

Malecón 663 is a trendy hotel located in the lively Vedado district. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, it offers comfortable accommodations for visitors seeking an exciting Havana experience. Stay in style and enjoy the energetic ambiance of this popular hotel.

El Candil Boutique

El Candil Boutique is a unique hotel in Havana that transforms from a daytime restaurant into a lively party hub, especially on Friday nights. With alfresco live music, often featuring big-name acts, it exudes an energetic atmosphere reminiscent of an undergraduate freshers' ball. This boutique hotel offers a lively and energetic atmosphere for guests seeking an unforgettable Havana experience.
